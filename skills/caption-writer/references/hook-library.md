# Hook Library

A working library of hook formulas by type. Use as a starting point — always adapt to the brand's voice and the specific post concept.

---

## Curiosity Hooks

- "I was wrong about [common belief]."
- "The real reason [outcome] happens isn't what most people think."
- "[Impressive result] — and it only took [surprisingly short time]."
- "Nobody talks about [thing]. Here's why that's a problem."
- "We tried [thing] for [time period]. Here's what actually happened."
- "This [simple thing] changed [outcome] completely."

---

## Story Hooks

- "Last [week/month], [unexpected thing] happened."
- "I almost [big mistake or failure]."
- "[Time period] ago, I [past state]. Today, [current state]."
- "A client came to us with [problem]. Here's what we found."
- "The first time I [did thing], I [honest reaction]."
- "We almost didn't [do thing]. Here's why I'm glad we did."

---

## Value / How-To Hooks

- "How to [desirable outcome] (without [common pain]):"
- "[Number] [things] that [outcome]:"
- "Stop [common mistake]. Do this instead:"
- "The [X]-step process we use to [outcome]:"
- "Most people do [thing] wrong. Here's the right way:"
- "Here's what [good outcome] actually looks like:"

---

## Contrarian Hooks

- "Unpopular opinion: [bold statement]."
- "[Common advice] is wrong. Here's why:"
- "I stopped [common practice] and [positive result]."
- "Everyone says [X]. I disagree."
- "The [industry] tells you to [thing]. Here's what they don't tell you:"
- "[Trendy thing] is overrated. This is what actually works."

---

## Question Hooks

- "What would you do if [scenario]?"
- "Is [common belief] actually true?"
- "Why does [frustrating thing] keep happening?"
- "What's the difference between [A] and [B]?"
- "Have you ever noticed [observation]?"
- "What's stopping you from [desirable outcome]?"

---

## Relatable / Observation Hooks

- "If you've ever [relatable experience], this is for you."
- "There's a reason [frustrating thing] keeps happening to you."
- "[Common situation] doesn't have to feel like [negative feeling]."
- "The moment I realised [insight] changed everything."
- "You don't need [expensive/complex thing]. You need [simple thing]."

---

## Result / Social Proof Hooks

- "[Client/customer] went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]."
- "We helped [type of client] achieve [specific result]."
- "[Specific result] in [timeframe]. Here's exactly what we did."
- "[Number] clients later, here's the one thing they all had in common."

---

## Timely / Seasonal Hooks

- "It's [season/time of year], which means [relevant observation]."
- "[Current event/trend] is changing [industry/topic]. Here's what it means for you."
- "With [holiday/occasion] coming up, now is the time to [action]."
- "Everyone's talking about [trend]. Here's the part they're missing."

---

## Hook Checklist

Before finalising any hook, check:
- [ ] Does it work as a standalone line, without any context?
- [ ] Would someone stop scrolling for this on a busy feed?
- [ ] Does it match the brand's voice (not just sound clever)?
- [ ] Does it set up the rest of the caption accurately?
- [ ] Is it free of AI filler phrases and generic openers?
